About Us

Fraser Valley Plumbing is a trusted plumbing company based in Fraser Valley, BC. Our experienced plumbers deliver quality workmanship, fast response times, and long-lasting solutions for all repairs, installations, and preventative maintenance jobs.

Whether you are dealing with an unexpected emergency, seeking a complete upgrade, or need a complex camera diagnostic inspection, our dedicated team is unconditionally committed to exceptional service and complete customer satisfaction.

Proudly Serving: Surrey, Abbotsford, Chilliwack, Cloverdale, Delta, Hope, Langley, Maple Ridge, and Port Coquitlam.

Our Services

Plumbing Services

Comprehensive layout diagnostics, basic repairs, structural pipe installations, and tailored fixes for residential and corporate systems.

Emergency Plumbing

Fast, reliable emergency response unit active round-the-clock to manage devastating bursts, severe overflows, and structural failures.

Leak Detection

Advanced electronic diagnostics pinpoint hidden slab and wall leaks efficiently before structural decay escalates.

Bathroom Plumbing

Expert installations and swift fixes for custom vanity faucets, toilet setups, shower modules, and tub mechanisms.

Kitchen Plumbing

Upgrades and service for sinks, modern garbage disposals, dishwashers, and specialized water filtration lines.

Camera Inspection

High-definition fiber-optic video evaluation to safely diagnose subterranean blockage layouts and pipeline cracks.

Drain Cleaning & Drainage

Clearing slow perimeter drains, stubborn blockages, and main stack pipelines with high-velocity hydro-jet methods.

Water Line Repair

Trenchless or targeted structural excavations addressing broken underground water utility link supplies directly.

Sewer Line Services

Comprehensive root extraction, structural re-lining, and complete replacements of compromised sewer mains.

Gas Fitting

Certified installation, safety pressure testing, and professional management of gas line appliances and range hooks.

Heating Services

Boiler tune-ups, forced-air furnace repairs, and energy-efficient heat pump optimization routines.

Cooling Services

Central air conditioner unit servicing, mini-split air systems installations, and cooling liquid balancing.

Plumbing Maintenance

Scheduled commercial and residential system flushes, pressure regulator valves testing, and preventative audits.

Residential Plumbing

Dedicated single and multi-family house diagnostics, updates, safety builds, and appliance integrations.

Commercial Plumbing

Heavy-duty corporate facilities backflow testing, grease trap logistics, and massive water loop designs.
